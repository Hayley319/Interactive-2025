The Legend of Nezha

"A hero's destiny is his own to shape."

Who is Nezha?

Nezha is one of the most famous mythological heroes in Chinese folklore. Known for his rebellious spirit and heroic destiny, Nezha is a young warrior who challenges fate and stands against oppression. He first appeared in the classic novel *Investiture of the Gods* (封神演义).

Nezha’s Rebirth (1961)

After sacrificing himself, Nezha was reborn using lotus roots. With a fire-tipped spear and wind-fire wheels, he became a warrior ready to fight against destiny.

Nezha’s Origin (1979)

Born from a lotus flower, Nezha was destined to be extraordinary. His father, General Li Jing, feared his uncontrollable power. When Nezha defeated the Dragon King’s son, Ao Bing, he faced divine punishment.

Nezha’s Symbolism (2019)

Nezha represents freedom, rebellion, and self-determination. His journey from a cursed child to a divine warrior reflects the power of choosing one's own path despite adversity.
