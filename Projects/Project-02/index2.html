Movie Summary

Nezha: Birth of the Demon Child (2019) is a Chinese animated fantasy film that reimagines the classic myth of Nezha. In this version, Nezha is born cursed, as a mix of the Demon Orb and the Spirit Pearl. Destined to bring destruction, he struggles against his dark fate and fights to prove that he can choose his own path.

The film is one of China’s highest-grossing animated movies and is known for its stunning animation, powerful storytelling, and humor. It introduces Nezha as a rebellious yet deeply emotional character, making it a fresh and modern take on the legendary tale.
