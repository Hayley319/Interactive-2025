Movie Summary

*Nezha Conquers the Dragon King* (1979) is a legendary animated film from **Shanghai Animation Film Studio**. It tells the story of Nezha, a powerful young warrior born from a lotus flower, who defies the oppressive Dragon King of the East Sea. After defeating the Dragon King's son, Ao Bing, Nezha sacrifices himself to protect his family. He is later reborn with divine powers and continues his fight against fate.

The film is renowned for its **traditional Chinese ink painting animation**, **epic battle sequences**, and **themes of rebellion and destiny**. It remains a **cultural milestone in Chinese animation**.
